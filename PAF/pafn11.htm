235. Eden Ludlow

Victor Ludlow's records show Eden's spouse as Mary Ann Crocket.t. Daniel H. Ludlow & Judy Edwards show her as Mary Ellen Crockett.

Dan Ludlow has a copy of the birth certificate and death certificate of Eden Ludlow.
Born 13 Oct 1886 at Boho, Violet Town, Vic.; son and eldest child of George Ludlow and Ellen White;
Died 19 Jun 1963, at Wodonga, Vic., [12 Burnley Street] at age 76, listed as dairy farmer who had lived "62 years in Victoria, 14 years in New South Wales";
Buried at Wodonga Cemetery, survived by two children: Elsie May, 48; Doris Evelyn, 46 and his wife Mary Ellen Crockett.

Mary Ellen Crockett

Daniel H. Ludlow has copy of Death Certificate: died 10 Oct 1966 at Wodonga, Vic., age 75 years after "61 years in Victoria, 14 years in New South Wales" buried at Wodonga Cemetery
