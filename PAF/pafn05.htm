49. Charles Ludlow

His name was listed on his father's death records from Hawthorn, Victoria, AUSTRALIA

Since Charles was listed as age 88 when he died 20 Sep 1939, and age 44 at his father's death 14 Feb 1896, he must have been born between 20 Sep 1851 and 14 Feb 1852. These dates match with the ages listed for his age at the time of arriving in Melbourne, his age at the time of his marriage and his age listed at the time of on his mother's death.

Charles arrived in Melbourne 30 May 1860 [age 8] on the ATLANTA.

Daniel H. Ludlow has a copy of Charles & Louisa Ann's marriage certificate: married 5 Mar 1884, at Tarawarra, Vic., he [32, brickmaker] lived at Hawthorn, she [23] at Tarawarra.

Daniel H. Ludlow has a copy of Charles Ludlow's death certificate: died 20 Sep 1939, at 4 Hastings Road, Hawthorn, Vic., 88 years of age, leaving 7 living children and 2 deceased, buried at Werribee Cemetery.

On his father's death certificate (John Ludlow 14 Feb 1896), Charles was listed as a surviving child, age 44.
On his mother, Mary Ann Underhill's death certificate, 4 Aug 1918 Charles was listed as a surviving child, age 67.

Louisa Ann Postlethwaite
